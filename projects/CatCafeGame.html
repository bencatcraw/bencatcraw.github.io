VR Cat Cafe Game

The Project

This game was created for the final for my AR/VR Development class. The challenge was to create a experience or game on a VR headset.

The Challenges

Some challenges I faced during this project was the time limit since we only had a month to finish this project while studying for all our other finals. This was also my first sizable online-multiplayer project and I struggled for a while understanding the networking solutions in unity and how to sync game data over the network.

The Outcome

The outcome of the project came out really well and I’m very happy with the progress after only working on it for a month. The idea of the game is similar to Overcooked, where players must receive orders from customers and make and deliver them. In this case, the players are seperated with one being in the kitchen and one at the register. The kitchen player has to make different kinds of toasts with spreads, while the register player has to receive, communicate, and deliver orders, while also pouring drinks.

My Impact

Because our class was a mix of 3d art students and computer science students, I was the only computer science student in my group. I learned a lot though doing that much work in engine alone and I’m grateful for my art students skills in modeling and texturing.
