## The Game
This game was created for my ICS 369 class which taught us the basics of unity development and design. Our game Mars Mech Mission, is a tower defense game about a lone dwarf (who kind of looks like Torbjorn from Overwatch) who has to defend his space base from robotic crabs and scorpions. In this game there is a grid system where you can place turrets and traps and upgrade them with materials you scavenge during the day. Once it turns night, the enemies attack and you have to keep your turrets from overheating throughout the night.

## My Impact
I worked in a group of 5 on this project and I was the sole programmer. I wrote all the scripts for the game including the enemy AI. I got to learn interesting ways to make new mechanics too I had never done before like a building system and upgradable abilities.
