**Brick Brick** is a mobile puzzle game where you connect bricks to clear area and score points. It can be played in the browser below.

Brick Brick was created in one night to test my development speed (and to make replicate a game my girlfriend plays that doesn't have ads.) Brick Brick is completely free and **[open source](https://github.com/bencatcraw/BrickBrick)** and is open to contributions!
